h3(highlights). Important changes

- <i class="icon-star"></i> Annotation driven activation is now **obsolote** := @@ArquillianSpecification@ revealed itself to be more problematic than valueable (see JIRA issues below for details). Therefore we make a breaking change and get rid of it in favour of custom test runner integration both Spock and Arquillian lifecycles. From now on you should simply decorate your specification with @@RunWith(ArquillianSputnik)@.
